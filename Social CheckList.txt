A social media checklist is to be followed by every person using virtual environment .
 DO's
1. maintain a professional profile
2.respect others online
3. be careful while sharing personal info/avoid sharing it
4. use strong passwords for all the applications
5. think before posting on any social media


DONT's

1. Don't share sensitive infos online (phone number , otps passwords)
2. Don’t engage in cyberbullying
3.Don’t post inappropriate content
4. Don’t spread misinformation
5. Don’t use weak or common passwords
